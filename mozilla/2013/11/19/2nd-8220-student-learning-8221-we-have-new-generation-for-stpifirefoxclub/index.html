2nd &#8220;Student Learning&#8221; : We have new generation for STPIFirefoxClub

ariestiyansyah
2013-11-19
Mozilla
FSA, STPIFirefoxClub, Student Learning

After the success of our first student learning last week we are come back with the next event, yes this is 2nd student learning. What happen in this week? Let me tell you..

The event starts at 4.00PM opened by our awesome Firefox Student Ambassador Rizal Praditya , commonly we talk about latest mozilla and Firefox Student Ambassador update before starting, good news this week many new college student (2013 generation) who participated.

I started to get into main topic, review about HTML and tell them about some project we need to contribute to mozilla. In the middle of the event all of FSA get the blank paper to be filled with their idea, cause Firefox is 9th in this year all FSA should write 9 idea for mozilla and our Firefox Club.

They are very serious write their ideas, and if you read their ideas there are many fun idea like “Mozilla paint my room with big firefox logo”, “Create Firefox Club anthem music”, “Mozilla give me Laptop, please!”, “GIVE US SNACK, PLEASE!”.

I also tell them about new contest from Mozilla Indonesia, contest are #AkudanFirefox and #FirefoxBatik with the DOORPRIZE go to Mountain View.

Next session is Q&A, in this session we also give rewards Firefox hat and Bag to our FSA, I think they are really happy with the rewards .

This is the end of this post, see you to next student learning #3 report .
