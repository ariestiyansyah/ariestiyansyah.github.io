Event and Recognition of STPI Firefox Club in 7 Month

ariestiyansyah
2019-02-27
Mozilla
FSA, Firefox Student Ambassador, STPI Firefox Club, STPIFirefoxClub, Student Learning

17 May 2013 I was created firefox club named STPI Firefox Club with purpose to spread firefox and promote Mozilla’s mission at Sekolah Tinggi Perpajakan Indonesia. Now this firefox club is 7 month, I am happy this club growing very fast and awesome so I’ll review what we do in 7 month with STPI Firefox Club.

Our Club has won the Affiliates Button contest on :

  • July
  • Agustus

Also most click of affiliates button in Indonesia on :

  • September
  • October
  • November

I hope we can win again in this December, some rewrads from FSA are Mozilla Baseball cap, Mozilla Bag, Moleskine Notebook, Firefox T-shirt and Mozilla Sweatshirt, more than 20 student get the rewards and they are happy.

Event we created until 19 December 2013 are :

  • Meetup & Buka Puasa bersama STPI Firefox Club – 25 July 2013
  • 1st Online Meeting on WhatsApp application ( Topic : Tactics and Strategy to run Firefox Club at our College School ) – 28 July 2013
  • STPI Firefox Club meeting ( Get involved with Firefox Student Ambassador ) – 7 October 2013
  • First “Student Learning” in @STPIFirefoxClub – 21 October 2013
  • 2nd Student Learning “New generation” – 18 November 2013
  • 3rd Student Learning “Get involved with Mozilla” – 25 November 2013
  • 4th Student Learning “Design Firefox OS App” – 09 December 2013
  • 5th Student Learning “Design Firefox OS App 2″ – 16 December 2013
